About BUP

Located in Dhaka, Bangladesh University of Professionals was established in 2008 and is committed to excellence in education and research. BUP is a unique institution that blends academic rigor with military discipline, ensuring a safe and structured learning environment.

Under the dynamic leadership of the Vice Chancellor, BUP has grown into one of the most prestigious public universities in the country. The VC sir's vision emphasizes quality education, ethical values, and innovation-driven research. With modern infrastructure and a student-friendly academic environment, BUP continues to shape future leaders for the nation and beyond.
